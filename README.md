Three results constrain the programme and must be addressed honestly.

**Cookson et al. (2026).** Wide binary star tests find no evidence for MOND-like modifications of gravity in the weak-field regime, consistent with standard GR and disfavoring theories that modify Newtonian dynamics at low acceleration.
